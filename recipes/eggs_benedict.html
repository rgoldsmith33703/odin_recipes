Eggs Benedict

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 4 slices Canadian bacon or thick-cut ham
  • 4 Perfectly Poached Eggs
  • 2 English muffins, split, toasted, and buttered
  • 1 recipe Hollandaise Sauce, kept warm
  • Dash of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Minced fresh parsley or chives (optional

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a 12-inch stainless steel or cast-iron skillet over medium heat until shimmering. Add the Canadian bacon (or ham) and cook, turning once, until browned on both sides, about 5 minutes. Transfer to a large plate and tent with foil to keep warm.

  2. Top each English muffin half with a slice of Canadian bacon, followed by a poached egg. Spoon some hollandaise sauce over the eggs, sprinkle with the cayenne pepper and herbs, if using, and serve immediately, passing the extra hollandaise in a warm bowl on the side.
